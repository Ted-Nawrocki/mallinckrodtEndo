Core filings, trust sites and AG summaries

Bankruptcy-related and settlement-related materials

  • Mallinckrodt Opioid Master Disbursement Trust II (MDT II) – official site Describes MDT II’s role as a “hub” trust, funding schedule and creditor trust structure for Mallinckrodt’s opioid settlements.
  • California Attorney General – National Opioids Settlements & opioid bankruptcies Summarizes Mallinckrodt’s and Endo’s opioid settlement obligations and trust arrangements from a state AG perspective.
  • North Carolina Attorney General – Mallinckrodt settlement details Details the structured payment framework and trust funding amounts for Mallinckrodt’s opioid resolution.
  • Endo Public Opioid Trust – official site Explains the Endo Public Opioid Trust’s beneficiaries, distribution schedule and governance.
  • Reuters – “Court OKs Mallinckrodt restructuring, $1 billion cut to opioid settlement” (Oct 10 2023) Describes court approval of Mallinckrodt’s second bankruptcy plan, including reduced opioid obligations and cancelled equity.
  • Davis Polk – “Mallinckrodt emerges from chapter 11” (Nov 16 2023) Summarizes the restructuring terms, take-back debt and new capital structure for Mallinckrodt.
  • Reuters – “Drugmaker Endo receives US court approval for bankruptcy restructuring” (Mar 19 2024) Reports on Endo’s plan confirmation, lender ownership and funding for the Endo Public Opioid Trust and other settlements.
  • U.S. Department of Justice – Endo Health Solutions opioid misbranding case (May 3 2024) Details the criminal fines and forfeiture related to misbranded Opana ER and how they relate to Endo’s bankruptcy resolution.
  • BioPharma Dive – “Mallinckrodt, beset by opioid drug suits, files for bankruptcy” (Oct 12 2020) Provides context on how opioid lawsuits and Acthar-related disputes contributed to Mallinckrodt’s first Chapter 11 filing.
  • Kentucky Attorney General – Opioid complaint against Endo Alleges deceptive marketing of Opana and Opana ER, outlining the state’s theory of Endo’s role in the opioid crisis.

Merger, spin-off and business-press coverage

News sources used for deal mechanics and narrative

  • Financial Times – “Mallinckrodt and Endo to combine after emerging from bankruptcy linked to opioid crisis” Frames the merger as a post-bankruptcy combination, with details on valuation, revenue, ownership split and opioid settlements.
  • Reuters – “Drugmakers Mallinckrodt and Endo to merge in nearly $7 billion deal” (Mar 13 2025) Reports on the announcement, enterprise value, expected revenue and NYSE listing plans.
  • MarketWatch – Mallinckrodt & Endo to combine in $6.7 billion deal Provides additional context on transaction structure and strategic rationale.
  • Investopedia – “Drugmakers Mallinckrodt, Endo to Merge, Plan NYSE Listing” Explains expected listing, enterprise value and pro forma portfolio mix.
  • Mallinckrodt.com – corporate site (Keenova & Par Health information) States the July 31 2025 merger date and November 2025 spin-off and rebrand to Keenova and Par Health.
  • Pharmaphorum – “Ireland’s Mallinckrodt closes spinout, rebrands as Keenova” (Nov 2025) Provides sales, headcount and strategic positioning for Keenova and Par Health.
  • BioSpace / PR Newswire – “Par Health completes spin-off from Mallinckrodt…” Describes Par Health’s focus on generics, APIs and sterile injectables and its manufacturing footprint.
  • FiercePharma – coverage of Keenova rebrand & Par Health spin-off Frames the combined group’s repositioning after opioid-related pressure and restructurings.
