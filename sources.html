Key sources used on this site

Factual statements in the overview, timeline and litigation pages are intended to be traceable to one or more of these links. This is a curated list, not an exhaustive bibliography.

Bankruptcy, settlements and trust structures
  • Mallinckrodt Opioid Master Disbursement Trust II (MDT II) – official site Describes MDT II’s role as a “hub” trust, its payment schedule and its relationships to sub-trusts and creditor classes.
  • California Attorney General – National Opioids Settlements & opioid bankruptcies Summarizes Mallinckrodt’s and Endo’s opioid settlement obligations and trust arrangements.
  • North Carolina Attorney General – Mallinckrodt settlement details Provides additional structure and payment information for Mallinckrodt’s opioid settlement framework.
  • Endo Public Opioid Trust – official site Explains the beneficiaries, distribution mechanics and governance of the Endo Public Opioid Trust.
  • Reuters – “Court OKs Mallinckrodt restructuring, $1 billion cut to opioid settlement” (Oct 10 2023) Details court approval of Mallinckrodt’s second plan, including reductions in opioid obligations and cancelled equity.
  • Davis Polk – “Mallinckrodt emerges from chapter 11” (Nov 16 2023) Summarizes Mallinckrodt’s post-emergence capital structure and take-back debt.
  • Reuters – “Drugmaker Endo receives US court approval for bankruptcy restructuring” (Mar 19 2024) Reports on Endo’s plan confirmation, lender ownership and settlement funding.
  • U.S. Department of Justice – Endo Health Solutions misbranding case (May 3 2024) Describes the criminal fines and forfeiture related to misbranded Opana ER.
  • BioPharma Dive – “Mallinckrodt, beset by opioid drug suits, files for bankruptcy” (Oct 12 2020) Provides context on how opioid lawsuits and Acthar-related disputes led to Mallinckrodt’s first Chapter 11 filing.
  • Kentucky Attorney General – Complaint against Endo Alleges deceptive marketing of Opana and Opana ER and sets out the state’s theory of Endo’s role in the opioid crisis.
Merger, spin-off and business-press coverage
  • Financial Times – “Mallinckrodt and Endo to combine after emerging from bankruptcy linked to opioid crisis” Frames the merger and provides details on valuation, opioid settlements and the companies’ post-bankruptcy context.
  • Reuters – “Drugmakers Mallinckrodt and Endo to merge in nearly $7 billion deal” (Mar 13 2025) Covers the announcement, enterprise value, expected revenue and NYSE listing plans.
  • MarketWatch – Mallinckrodt & Endo to combine in $6.7 billion deal Provides additional details on structure and rationale of the transaction.
  • Investopedia – “Drugmakers Mallinckrodt, Endo to Merge, Plan NYSE Listing” Explains the expected listing, enterprise value and pro forma portfolio.
  • Mallinckrodt.com – corporate site (Keenova / Par Health information) States the July 31 2025 merger closing date and November 2025 spin-off and rebrand into Keenova and Par Health.
  • Pharmaphorum – “Ireland’s Mallinckrodt closes spinout, rebrands as Keenova” (Nov 2025) Provides pro forma sales, headcount and positioning for Keenova and Par Health.
  • BioSpace / PR Newswire – “Par Health completes spin-off from Mallinckrodt…” Describes Par Health’s generics, API and sterile injectables focus and manufacturing footprint.
  • FiercePharma – coverage of Keenova rebrand & Par Health spin-off Frames the combined group’s repositioning after opioid-related pressures and restructurings.
